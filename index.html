About Us

The Cooperative That's Changing Rural Uganda

For over 50 years, Kimwanyi Dairy Farmers' Cooperative has been a beacon of hope and empowerment for vulnerable communities in Nakaseke, Uganda. Located just a two-hour drive from Kampala, we are dedicated to uplifting women, youth, and the elderly through sustainable agricultural practices and community support.

Empower

Supporting Vulnerable Members of Our Community

At Kimwanyi Dairy Farmers' Cooperative, we are dedicated to uplifting vulnerable members of our community, including women, youth, and the elderly. Our programs provide essential resources and training to help them thrive.

Empowering Women Farmers

90% of our members are women who lead our enterprises and receive leadership training, financial literacy, and market access support.

Developing Young Talent

We equip young farmers with modern techniques, entrepreneurship skills, and technology access to build sustainable farming careers

Supporting Our Elders

Our elderly members receive specialized support, and meaningful roles that honor their wisdom and experience.

Key Sectors

Transforming Lives Through Agriculture

Our three flagship programs - Coffee, Maize, and Dairy - create complete value chains that empower farmers from seed to market. Through comprehensive training, quality inputs, and guaranteed market access, we're building prosperity in rural communities.

COFFEE: Complete Value Chain Support

From seedling to cup - we guide coffee farmers through cultivation, processing, quality control, and direct market connections for premium prices.

MAIZE: Processing and Market Access

Boosting yields through improved seeds, modern techniques, and collective marketing that secures better prices for our maize farmers.

DAIRY: Quality Breeds and Best Practices

Quality breeds, best practices training, and milk collection systems that ensure consistent income for our dairy farmers.
